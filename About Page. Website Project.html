About Creative Minds

Creative Minds is about helping beginning artists start their creative path. We help new artists by providing drawing, painitng, and designing tutorials with our quality videos and images. We believe anyone can draw, paint, or design and this is the best place to learn. We offer video tutorials, helpful images, classes that are free to take online. Since we have opened our website, we've helped thousands of art students, college students, and homeschooled students with their art skills. AI might take over art careers but there are still people who have a strong passion for art and want their work to be shared and come to life. :)

We launched our website in 2016 and over the years we've helped thousands of clients coming to learn new art and design techniques. The amazing people on our team are also skilled artist and college graduatues, to help beginners with their art skills and put toegther their professional portfolios for job interviews. We have a variety of tutorials and topics that interest your style of art.
